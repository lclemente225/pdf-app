Instructions for signing

  1. Click on "Create Signature". You can write your signature there.
  2. Click on "Save Signature". The window will close.
  3. If you need to reset your signature, you can click on "Clear Signature"
  4. Make sure you check the "Insert Signature" checkbox if you want to sign the pdf.
  5. Click on the pdf to sign. The signature will appear right above your mouse cursor.
